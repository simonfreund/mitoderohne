mit oder ohne is a series of photographs taken by Simon Freund with an old Polaroid 600 camera between the end of 2019 and the beginning of 2020. The series is composed of 32 motives each taken with and without flash to form a pair of two. The result is a 64-part memory game printed on original Ravensburger memory cards and this online version of the same game. Play with 64, 36 or 16 cards
